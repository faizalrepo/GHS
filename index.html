Welcome to the Ghostly Debug Exorcism

In a quaint and forgotten town named Puzzleton, nestled in a valley surrounded by misty mountains, a legend was told of an eerie place known as the "Crypt of the Lost Souls." Locals believed it was a portal to the afterlife, and many had ventured in but never returned. However, a group of daring individuals gathered, drawn by the allure of unraveling the mysteries that shrouded this enigmatic crypt.
